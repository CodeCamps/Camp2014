Haunted House Crashers

A side-scrolling smash'em up, similar to Castle Crashers. The current design supports one to four players.

The players are kids who are scared by a man in a hockey mask, and run into a haunted house. Once in the house, they take on the persona of their choosing - warrior, mage, monk, or archer. Each class has its own strengths and weaknesses.

The group works their way through the house, battling all sorts of baddies. In the final battle, you have to defeat the man in the mask to complete the game.
